Welcome to IGCE

Indra Ganesan College of Engineering (IGCE) was established in 2008 with a vision to provide quality technical education and produce skilled engineers who can contribute to society and industry.

Located in the heart of Tiruchirappalli, Tamil Nadu, IGCE is affiliated to Anna University and accredited by NAAC with a B+ grade. Our institution is committed to academic excellence, innovation, and holistic student development.

We offer undergraduate and postgraduate programs across various engineering disciplines, supported by state-of-the-art infrastructure, experienced faculty, and strong industry connections.

Our Vision

To be a center of excellence in engineering education, research, and innovation, producing globally competent professionals who contribute to technological advancement and societal welfare.

Our Mission

  • Provide quality education with industry-relevant curriculum
  • Foster research, innovation, and entrepreneurship
  • Develop ethical and socially responsible engineers
  • Build strong industry-academia partnerships

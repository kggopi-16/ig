Admission Process

1

Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria for your desired program. Minimum 50% in 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.

2

Fill Application Form

Complete the online application form with accurate details and upload required documents (10th, 12th certificates, photos).

3

Pay Application Fee

Submit the application fee of ₹1,000 through online payment gateway. Fee is non-refundable.

4

Attend Counseling

Attend the counseling session based on TNEA rank. Bring original certificates for verification.

5

Confirm Admission

Pay the admission fee to confirm your seat. Complete all formalities and receive your admission letter.

Eligibility Criteria

UG Programs (B.E / B.Tech)

  • Passed 10+2 or equivalent with minimum 50% aggregate
  • Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics mandatory
  • Valid TNEA rank or JEE Main score
  • Age: 17-25 years as on Dec 31, 2024

PG Programs (M.E / M.Tech)

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant engineering field
  • Minimum 50% aggregate in UG (45% for SC/ST)
  • Valid GATE score preferred
  • No age limit for PG programs
